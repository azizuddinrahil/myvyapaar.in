How to generate GSTR-1 using Vyapar?

  • by Vyapar
  • April 22, 2019October 12, 2020


Registered under regular GST?

If your answer is YES, you must file your GSTR-1 returns by 10th of every month.

Preparing GSTR-1 yourself manually may be difficult. But, business accounting software like Vyapar lets you do it with ease in just seconds

Here are 5 EASY steps to generate GSTR-1 using Vyapar:

Step 1: Open your Vyapar App, swipe in the Left Menu, click on Reports, scroll down to reach GSTR Reports & tap on GSTR-1.

Step 2: If you want to generate GSTR-1 for the first quarter of this year, choose “Jan 2019” for FROM and “April 2019” for TO field.

Step 3: Now, select the firm’s name.

Step 4: Your GSTR-1 report is READY. You can view all your Sale and Sale Returns for the given duration.

Step 5: Download, share or view this report in PDF or Excel format.

Step 6: Upload this GSTR-1 report to the GST portal (https://www.gst.gov.in/) to complete the return filing process.

Advantages of generating your GSTR-1 report using Vyapar:

  1. You will save a lot of time and effort.
  2. You need No accounting knowledge.
  3. Your GSTR-1 return does not get easily rejected on GST portal.
  4. You can share your GSTR-1 return easily with your accountant for verification.
